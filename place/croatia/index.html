69 National Statistics
URL: http://www.dzs.hr/
Format: pdf

Regarding up-to date status of data, the documents are available, but some are very slow processed. It is currently (as of November 2014.) unclear whether the data from the 2011 census is fully published.

Census data is available in a machine-readable format (MS Excel), but most periodical publications are not (available only in PDF or HTML).

Reproduction in whole or in part in any form or medium of any of CBS publications without acknowledging the Croatian Bureau of Statistics as the source is prohibited.

36 Pollutant Emissions
URL: http://zrak.mzoip.hr
Format: html

This database differs from the one reported upon in 2013, since it contains aggregate nationwide data, as opposed to single pollutant sources (e.g., this database provides information on aggregate emissions in the city of Rijeka, instead of those by different companies nationwide). In addition, this database is updated on a daily basis, whereas the one reported upon in 2013 reports on an annual basis and does not currently (November 7 2014) contain data for 2014.

Data is copyrighted by the Ministry of Environment and Nature Protection.
