7 Location datasets
URL: http://www.iompost.com/postal/postcodes.xml
Format: HTML

The Isle of Man Government still owns the Isle of Man Post Office. Postcode searches require a registered email address (free) and rate limited to 10 searches per day.

The government IT department sells the postcode/geolocation dataset to a very small customer base of couriers, dairy co-operative doorstep milk delivery service, Tesco, etc.

An IT services company (http://www.afd.co.uk/) also resell geolocation datasets and data cleansing services.

URL: http://www.gov.im/publictransport/

Requested CSV or XLS data from public transit director but (apparently) a legacy system without export option, a significant internal IT department fee to scope and/or export any data from any system and an ongoing bus drivers strike dominates management time. I have handcoded most routes into GTFS from printed timetables to reuse with next bus movile apps, Mapumental, etc. via openmindedly.
