13 Transport Timetables
URL: http://www.railway.gov.bd/all_ic_mail_shedule.asp


Management of public transport varies in Bangladesh. While rail communication is fully run and managed by the government, the road and water transport is mostly managed by the private sector where the government-run services are also available. Timetable for passenger and goods services of railway are publicly available, so is the government-run Airlines and public buses. Time schedule of transport services operated by the private sector are not publicly available, though. Cannot find licence information.
